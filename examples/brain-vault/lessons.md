# Lessons

## Respect the first invalidation

If the stop was placed from the thesis, moving it after entry usually means the thesis changed and the journal did not. Close or re-plan.

## Thin markets punish impatience

Prediction markets and builder assets can show attractive prices with poor depth. Use limits, size smaller, and expect partial fills.

## Re-read exposure before adding risk

Before every write, check current positions, open orders, and venue collateral. The next trade is never isolated from existing exposure.
